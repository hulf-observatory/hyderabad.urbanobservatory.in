Hyderabad Municipal Survey, 1915 by Leonard Munn

Explore the historical municipal survey maps of Hyderabad conducted by Leonard Munn between 1911 and 1913, after the devastating 1908 floods.

Toggle between the map and the base image to visualise changes over the last 100 years. Notice areas like Mir Jumla Tank, now fully built over, and the development around RTC X Roads and other parts of the city. Key locations have been marked to help you orient yourself. We’ll be adding more stories and archival images soon. If you have stories or photos that capture how these areas have changed, we’d love to hear from you.
